The Showroom

Instruments of superior design, rebuilt to their highest potential.

Every piano in Ed's showroom has been carefully evaluated, selected, and rebuilt to meet the LightHammer standard. These are instruments from the finest American and European makers, each one engineered and customized to bring out its highest possible tonal and mechanical capability.

Ed specializes in Steinway, Mason & Hamlin, Chickering, Baldwin, and other instruments of exceptional original design. Each piano receives his complete rebuilding process, including LightHammer Tone Regulation, and is ready to be played and enjoyed for decades to come.

Availability changes as instruments are completed and sold. Contact Ed directly to discuss current inventory, commission a specific rebuild, or bring your own instrument to the shop for evaluation.
