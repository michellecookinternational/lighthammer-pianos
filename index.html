Sound Innovation

A LightHammer piano wears in, not out.

Ed McMorrow's proprietary tone regulation creates instruments with a lighter, more responsive touch that translates the pianist's expression with uncommon fidelity. The tone is warmer, more dynamic, and more durable.

Clients who have played Ed's pianos for over forty years report that the tone and touch remain stable, the actions are nearly indestructible, and the instrument continues to reward musical intention decade after decade. Every piano Ed rebuilds is engineered and customized to bring out the highest possible capability of that specific instrument.
